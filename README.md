## ğŸ“Š What We Found

We looked at different things to understand churn:

### 1. Who is leaving?
*   **Senior Citizens:** Older people are more likely to leave than younger ones. They might need simpler plans or better support.
*   **Gender:** Men and women leave at about the same rate, so gender isn't a big factor.

### 2. Services
*   **Fiber Optic Internet:** Customers with Fiber Optic internet leave more often. This is strange because it's a premium service. There might be a problem with the price or the connection quality.

### 3. Contracts and Payments
*   **Month-to-Month Contracts:** People with monthly contracts leave the most.
*   **Long-Term Contracts:** People with 1-year or 2-year contracts stay much longer.
*   **Payment Method:** Customers who pay by "Electronic Check" leave more often.

### 4. New Customers
*   **First Year Risk:** Most people who leave do so in the first few months. If a customer stays for a year, they are likely to stay for a long time.
